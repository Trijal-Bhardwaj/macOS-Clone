Cause a Stir! Cause a Stir!

Cause a Stir!

You’re the owner of a café for Pokémon in this fun and casual game. To solve puzzles, you must link matching Pokémon icons to clear them from the screen. Each puzzle has its own objectives, so think carefully before making your move.

Be on the lookout for ingredients appearing on the puzzle board. New recipes bring new ingredients, and you’ll need different strategies to clear them all.

Staff Pokémon work at your café, and they’re always eager to help! Use their special Café Skills to help clear the puzzles.
